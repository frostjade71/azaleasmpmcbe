Season 1

FrostworldSMP - How It All Started

May 2024

FrostworldSMP was the very first season, created by Frost on a Minecraft Bedrock Realm. It became a nostalgic place for many loyal players who still remember it today. The realm grew with many people joining, building, and playing together.

The McMan Bridge

One of the most famous builds was the McMan Bridge, made by a player named McMan. It connected the spawn area to the trading hub. Many people griefed the bridge and even fought over it, but McMan always fixes it. Since then, no one heard from him again. To honor him, the players re-design the bridge and peace has brought the players closer.

The First PvP Champion

During this season, the first PvP tournament was held. GingeBiscuit135 won and became the first champion. He showed great skill and determination playing in the realm.

The Fall of Frostworld

Sadly, the season didn't end well. A mystery player used hacks to crash the realm by spamming laggy items and flooding the chat. Even though Frost tried to fix it with backups, nothing worked. The world was too broken to save. In the end, Frost decided to start a new world. Season 1 came to a close, but its memories still lives on. Goodbye FrostWorld.

Season 2

Emerald (The New Azalea)

June 5 2024

The season renamed FrostworldSMP to AzaleaSMP with the themed: Emerald as a tribute to the addon Azalea Essentials, created by TrashyDaFox. The addon brought unique features perfect for hosting any Minecraft realm or server, making it an essential tool for smoother gameplay and better management. The spawn area stood out with its bright green emerald tiles and modern architecture

A Peaceful and Growing Community

Though there were no major events like PvP tournaments, players still enjoyed building, exploring, and teaming up. It was a peaceful time where the community focused on fun and collaboration. One unique trend among players during this season was the widespread practice of villager kidnapping!

The Rise of the Community

With the help of Gordy, Frost's close friend and the appointed Co-Founder, the Discord server grew quickly—reaching over 200 members. As the community expanded, so did the realm. Season 2 marked the beginning of something great. Welcome to Azalea!

Season 3

The Forgotten World

June 14 2024

Season 3 introduced a new era—The Forgotten World. This season brought a medieval-themed world full of ruins, castles, and treasure. From the very start, some of the most active and pre-invited players joined, setting the stage for all pvp hungry players.

Loot, Power, and Progress

Players quickly explored the world, finding loot hidden across different regions. Some became incredibly wealthy within the first day, crafting full Netherite gear in under 24 hours. Ruins, abandoned homes, and old castles became hotspots for rare items and hidden gear.

Tournaments and Champions

This season also had two PvP tournaments, making it the most event-filled season yet. Two champions rose from the battlefield: Heemois and Aspen the Quev. The Champions of the Forgotten World!

Season 4

New Horizon

July 14 2024

For the first time in AzaleaSMP history, Season 4 was hosted on a Minecraft server instead of a realm. This marked a major step forward and a opening for new possibilities.

The Season Builders

The season featured some of the most dedicated builders ever seen in the SMP. The building team included: Leosambdf (now known as ShadowHZ13), GingeBiscuit135, GoldLearner, defdroid8008, K3ithKOn, RioIsBetter982, and the server's leaders, Gordy and Frost.

Together, they created amazing structures that welcomed players into a vibrant and exciting world.

Unexpected Pauses

On July 22, 2024, Frost had to pause the server due to busy academic responsibilities. But on July 31, he made a comeback announcement—New Horizons would continue, now with a brand-new Navigation UI feature to improve gameplay.

Sadly, the server was suspended again on August 16, 2024, as Frost couldn't manage it during that time. Frost had plans for a magical, fantasy-themed Season 5 called Uncharted, but it was ultimately cancelled. With that, AzaleaSMP came to an end—closing a beloved chapter for everyone who had been part of the journey.

Season 5

Awakened

April 14 2025

After 7 months of silence, Frost made a surprise return to the Discord server on March 26, 2025, with exciting news—AzaleaSMP was officially coming back. The revival was set for April 14, 2025 as a realm, and it brought back the heart and spirit of what made AzaleaSMP special.

One of the Longest Seasons

Season 5 lasted for 43 days, making it one of the longest-running season in Azalea history. Lets goooo!!

The Building Competition

The first ever building contest in the SMP saw players recreating and showcasing their best base designs. Naydzgabi took the crown with an elegant French house build, scoring 9.5/10 from the judges.

The PvP Tournament

A thrilling battle between 5 skilled warriors. After intense fights, yourshrk12146 emerged victorious, earning the title of Season 5's PvP Champion.

Player of the Season

A community poll was held to decide the "Player of the Season." Kaiza5234 garnered a total of 14 votes, earning the title of Season 5's "Player Of The Season".

Season Memories

The season was fulfilled with friendships, cooperation, PvP dwellers lurking, and miners seeking wealth. The season so far was a productive one. AzaleaSMP is more than a realm—it's a community.
